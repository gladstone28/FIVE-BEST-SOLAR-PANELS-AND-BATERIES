# Quick pairing tips

Typical starter package: 6--8 kW of panels + 10--20 kWh storage + hybrid
inverter.

# Jamaica-specific notes

-   Favor panels with IEC 61701 salt-mist certification.
-   Use high-wind-rated racking and stainless fasteners.
-   Choose brands with active Caribbean installer networks.

# Recommendations

-   Premium efficiency: Maxeon 6 + Tesla Powerwall 3
-   Value + scalability: Qcells Q.TRON or Canadian Solar HiKu + BYD HVM
    or Generac PWRcell
-   All-in-one: Panasonic panels + EVERVOLT 2.0
